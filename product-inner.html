DESCRIPTION:

Comline work closely with a top provider in the manufacture of brake discs & pads allowing them to create products to a high standard and specification.

They believe it is essential when designing to start with the right materials - before the production of the final product, these materials are tested for tolerances and resistance to high temperature, which can occur in the friction of braking.

Brake discs & pads work along side brake calipers, each does a job to make sure your vehicle brakes precisely and effectively, for the safety of you and others on the road.

It’s essential that your brakes are working to the highest of their ability. Harsh braking and a braking system left without regular checks can become worn, which will lead to reduced braking friction and this can be extremely dangerous.

Please check if your vehicle has solid or vented discs before ordering, if you are unsure Click Here. The difference is very easy to spot even to the untrained eye.
